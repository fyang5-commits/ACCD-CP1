3D Scene Proposal

For my Blender project, I’m planning to create a 3D background scene that connects with my online doctor scheduling app. I want to show the interface in a realistic environment, like how a patient might actually use it at home.

The main idea is to build a cozy home office setup — a wooden desk with a laptop showing the dashboard, a mug, a small plant, and some warm daylight coming through the window. The goal is to make the scene feel calm, comfortable, and believable.

The reference images I collected show a mix of modern home offices, small workspace corners, and casual desk setups. I like the idea of keeping it simple but polished, so the focus stays on the interface while the environment adds a soft storytelling touch.
